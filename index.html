Major changes are being proposed by the province for what can be built in Toronto.

The Province has issued proposed amendments to the regulation of Garden Suites and Secondary Suites (otherwise known as Additional Resident Units or ARU).

This will apply to development that is four storeys or less - basically anywhere in our neighbourhoods.

There are many residents association Groups in cities across the province. These groups can be coalitions, federations or confederations. The annual fee for Group Membership is $50.

All of the residents' associations who belong to a Group are automatically granted Associate Memberships in FUN.
