Welcome to the official website of the Craven Road Residents Association.


The Craven Road Residents Association (CRRA) is a community organization that represents the interests of residents living on Craven Road in Toronto, Ontario. Our mission is to preserve the unique character of our neighbourhood, promote community engagement, and advocate for responsible development.
